FAQ

Answers for operators and field teams.

How long does implementation take?

Most teams launch in under three weeks. We import your catalog, configure install rules, and connect ServiceTitan or Jobber before enabling the checkout block.

Can we sync to multiple brands or regions?

Yes—Fitted supports multi-storefront setups with regionalized pricing, availability pools, and lead routing per brand.

What about existing scheduling tools?

Fitted keeps ServiceTitan, Jobber, and Calendly calendars as the source of truth. We sync install jobs, customer info, and updates both ways.

How do we measure success?

The control center tracks attach rate, cycle time, and crew utilization so you can see ROI within the first month.
