FAQ

Answers for busy operators.

How is Fitted different from Angi or HipPages?

Timing. We capture customers at their highest intent—immediately after buying an installation-heavy product. They aren’t comparison shopping; they need help now.

What if an installer does a bad job?

Every installer is licensed, insured, and maintains a 4.5★+ rating. Jobs are covered by $5M liability insurance and poor performers are removed automatically.

How long does setup take?

Five to ten minutes. Install the Shopify app, select which products offer installation, and you’re live. We handle installer verification and matching.

Do we manage the installers?

No. They are independent professionals with their own booking and payment systems. You simply offer the connection—zero operational overhead.

What products perform best with Fitted?

Anything that needs professional installation: lighting, ceiling fans, appliances, heat pumps, air conditioners, outdoor equipment, mirrors, TVs, security systems, and more.

How do installers get paid?

Customers pay installers directly through their booking system. Fitted bills the installer $25–30 per lead via Stripe after the booking is confirmed.
